About Bombastic Bookstore

Welcome to Bombastic Bookstore Inventory Management Tool! Here, you can easily track all books within your inventory. Functionalities include (and are not limited to) adding new items, removing items, managing existing entries, and various user settings. Our goal is to provide you with an intuitive and efficient web-based tool to streamline inventory management, making sure that you always have the most accurate information. Navigate through the main menu to begin.

Key Features

  • Real-Time Inventory Tracking: Keep up-to-date information of the entire stock.
  • Easy Data Management: Adding, updating, and removing items is just a few clicks away!
  • Search and Filter: Quickly find books by title, ISBN, or author.
  • Easy-to-use Interface: Bombastic's interface is intuitive and simple to use.

Decision Making

Our backend for Bombastic is written primarily in Python and stood up by Django web framework, while our database is SQL. We chose Django for its scalability and ease-of-use, with the intention that we would be able to pass on the efficiency to the user. By design, Bombastic is also scalable, allowing the customer to grow or shrink comfortably with minimal effort. Bombastic is also highly modular, which feeds into scalability. Entire pages can be added easily and quickly to the main web application. We decided on a SQL database primarily for the structured data storage and vertical scalability. Ultimately, we chose tools that reflected our goals.

Why Bombastic?
